## Story

As an Avid Listener,
I want to search for a book by title and save it to my Reading List in one action,
So that a recommendation I just heard doesn't get lost.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

### AC-1
Given a Reader on the home screen, when they type a book title and submit the search, then they see a list of matching books with title, author, and cover image.

### AC-2
Given search results are displayed, when a Reader taps "Save" on a book, then the book appears at the top of their Reading List and the button changes to "Saved".

### AC-3
Given a Reader submits a search that returns no results, when the search completes, then they see "No books found for [title]" and a prompt to try a different title.

### AC-4
Given a Reader submits a search while offline, when the request fails, then they see "No internet connection. Check your connection and try again."
